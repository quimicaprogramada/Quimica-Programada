Welcome to Química Programada

The objective of Química Programada is to share practical applications using Python, HTML, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, focused on the fields of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering. There are few open-source websites that provide examples related to these areas to illustrate how techniques such as Object-Oriented Programming, Data Science, HTML forms, and databases with PHP and MySQL can be useful for solving real-world problems. Most websites and courses tend to focus on examples from business, e-commerce, logistics, and similar fields. These exercises were developed in parallel with programming courses taken by the developer of Química Programada, with the goal of reinforcing the course content. Comments, feedback, or suggestions for improvement can be sent to quimicaprogramada@gmail.com. The profile will be updated as soon as new relevant applications are developed.
